## Hire me! 👋

I'm a software engineer with a background in trust & safety, finance
operations, consumer banking contact centers, and higher education
administration.

Previously I . . .

* Designed, implemented, and improved business processes, balancing customer
  experience, efficiency, risk, and sustainability.
* Created sustainable reporting processes and dashboards.
* Had over a decade of experience working with BPO and contact center vendors.
* Have been industry agnostic - the important thing is an interesting
  challenge. So far I have specific experience in Trust & Safety, finance
  operations, cloud computing fraud and abuse, consumer banking contact center
  operations, and higher education admissions.

In my spare time, I'm working on [GovQL](https://govql.us), a GraphQL API to
make US legislative data (congresspeople, legislation, votes, etc.) easily
queryable.
